# 📱 How to Use WeSpend

**WeSpend** helps you track your money effortlessly — even offline.
From logging expenses to understanding your spending habits, everything is designed to be simple and clear.

---

# 🏁 1. Getting Started

### Set up your accounts

When you first open the app, you’ll choose your accounts:

- Cash
- GCash / Maya
- Bank accounts (BPI, BDO, etc.)

👉 You can add more later anytime.

---

# 💳 2. Understanding Accounts

Each account represents where your money is:

- **Debit accounts** → Cash, banks, e-wallets
- **Credit accounts** → Credit cards (tracked as debt)

### What you’ll see:

- Balance
- Available funds
- Credit usage (for credit accounts)

---

# ➕ 3. Adding Transactions

Tap the **➕ (Add button)**

### Choose type:

- **Income** → Money coming in
- **Expense** → Money going out
- **Transfer** → Move money between accounts

---

### Example:

- `500 food` → Expense
- `15000 salary` → Income
- Transfer between accounts

---
